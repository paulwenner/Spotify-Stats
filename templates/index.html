Description

With our application, you can explore your Spotify statistics and obtain a wealth of information about your music preferences. You can easily view your top songs and artists and select the time period you wish to analyze.

Our application uses the Spotify API in conjunction with the PKCE Authorization Workflow to ensure the security of your data and provide you with a seamless experience.
